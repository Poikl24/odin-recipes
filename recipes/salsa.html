Spicy Salsa

Description

A salsa so hot that it won an award. From a reddit post

Ingredients

  • 1/4lb of Habenero or Scotch Bonnet
  • 1/4lb of Red Chile Peppers (Fresno Chiles)
  • 1/4lb of Serrano Peppers
  • 1/4lb of Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Carton of Cherry or Plum Tomatoes
  • 1 Large Vidalia Onion
  • 2 Cups of Water
  • 32oz of Orange Juice
  • 2 Tablespoons of Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Tablespoon of Chipotle Powder
  • 2 Tablespoons of Smoked Paprika
  • Smoked Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop and saute veggies with a splash of oil at high heat about 5 minutes, then reduce to medium for an additional ten minutes.
  2. Add water and orange juice, and bring to a rolling boil for 30 minutes.
  3. Add smoked salt, pepper, chipotle powder, paprika, and vinegar
  4. Reduce heat to medium low and puree with immersion blender
  5. Reduce to desired thickness lowering heat as needed, about three hours.
  6. (Optional) Let it sit overnight.
